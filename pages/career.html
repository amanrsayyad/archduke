Project Manager

Contact Info

info@archdukeconstructions.com

Job description

As a Project Coordinator, you will support the pre-construction phase of projects, working closely with project managers, estimators, and clients. You will play a key role in ensuring the successful planning and organization of construction projects, from initial concept through to execution.

Key responsibilities
  • Assist in the preparation and coordination of pre-construction activities, including budgeting. scheduling, and procurement.
  • Support the project team by maintaining project documentation, tracking progress, and ensuring all paperwork is accurate and up-to-date.
  • Coordinate communication between clients, subcontractors, and the internal project team.
  • Prepare and distribute project reports, meeting minutes, and other relevant documentation.
The successful applicant
  • Proven Experience: A minimum of 8 years of experience as a Project Coordinator.
  • Educational Background: Bachelor's degree in Construction Management or a related field.
  • Industry Knowledge: Thorough understanding of the construction industry and relevant regulations.
  • Organizational Skills: Strong organizational and time management skills to manage projects.
  • Problem-Solving Skills: Excellent analytical and problem-solving abilities.
